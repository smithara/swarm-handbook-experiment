GRACE_x_MAG

Magnetic measurements derived from GRACE platform magnetometers

Thematic areas: Magnetic measurements

Applicable missions: GRACE

This product contains calibrated magnetic measurements derived from GRACE platform magnetometers

B_NEC_raw nT calibrated and aligned magnetic vector in NEC (North, East, Center) frame 3 CDF_DOUBLE
