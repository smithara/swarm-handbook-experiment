SW_MAGx_CA_1B

Magnetic vector and scalar data for monitoring and calibration purposes

Thematic areas: Magnetic measurements

Applicable missions: Swarm

Applicable spacecraft: Swarm-A, Swarm-B, Swarm-C

Description

The MAGX_CA_1B Product contains magnetic vector and scalar data for monitoring, TCF.VFM verification, and calibration purposes. It contains raw as well as processed VFM vector measurements, VFM temperatures, and fully converted and corrected ASM measurements at 1 Hz rate. This is essentially the data used for the TCF.VFM estimation but including also the raw VFM data.
